Cencori is the **Backbone of Intelligence**. 

We provide the complete infrastructure layer for the intelligence era — the same way AWS was the foundation of the internet and Stripe was the foundation of mobile commerce. 

In the intelligence era, building a product shouldn't mean spending 6 months solving "plumbing" before writing a single line of application code. Cencori unifies the fragmented AI stack into a single, cohesive platform.

## Defining Intelligence

At Cencori, we don't just build for "AI." We build for **Intelligence** — the capacity of any system to perceive, reason, decide, and act. To us, intelligence is the intersection of three frontiers:

- **Software**: LLMs, autonomous agents, and intelligence-first applications.
- **Hardware**: Custom silicon, edge computing, and smart devices.
- **Robotics**: Autonomous drones, self-driving systems, and mechatronics.

Whether you are shipping a B2B SaaS platform, an autonomous drone fleet, or a smart city infrastructure, Cencori provides the backbone that makes that intelligence possible.

Intelligence is the most transformative force in technology. The infrastructure that powers it should be open, complete, and accessible to every builder on earth. 

Cencori solves the three primary gaps in AI production:
- **The Infrastructure Gap**: Unified access to 100+ models with native training (Compute) and persistence (Memory).
- **The Security Gap**: Real-time jailbreak detection, PII masking, and audit trails baked into every request.
- **The Economic Gap**: Native end-user billing that turns AI from a cost center into a high-margin business.

---

## Products

Cencori is a platform of platforms. Each product is built to stand alone, but they achieve compounding value when used together on the same backbone:

1. **AI Gateway**: A secure, unified endpoint for 100+ models with dynamic routing, automatic failover, and enterprise-grade security.
2. **Billing**: The economic engine. Meter, limit, and charge your users for AI usage automatically with native Stripe integration.
3. **Compute**: GPU-backed infrastructure for training, fine-tuning, and low-latency inference.
4. **Memory**: Persistent context for AI agents. Vector storage, RAG pipelines, and long-term user profiles.
5. **Deployment**: Ship intelligent systems from cloud to chip with serverless endpoints and edge deployment.
6. **Data**: The data flywheel. Explore, clean, version, and monetize training datasets.

## Why Cencori?

- **One SDK, One Key**: Access every Cencori product through a single, unified interface.
- **Provider Agnostic**: Switch between OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and open-source models with a single line of code.
- **Built for Production**: SOC2-ready architecture with tamper-proof logs and real-time observability.
- **Monetization by Default**: Stop worrying about API costs. Charge your users exactly what they consume, with your own custom markups.

## The Power of the Backbone

Cencori doesn't just "route" requests. It manages the entire lifecycle of an intelligent action.

```typescript
import { Cencori } from 'cencori';

const cencori = new Cencori();

// Gateway + Security + Billing in one call
const response = await cencori.ai.chat({
  model: 'claude-3-5-sonnet',
  messages: [{ role: 'user', content: 'Scan this medical record...' }],
  security: { scanPII: true, jailbreakDetection: true },
  billing: { userId: 'user_456', plan: 'pro-tier' }
});

console.log(response.content);
```
